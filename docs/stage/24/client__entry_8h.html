◆ client_protocol_t

enum client_protocol_t

An RDMnet client protocol.

Enumerator
kClientProtocolRPT 

An RPT client.

RPT clients implement the RDM functionality of RDMnet, and are further divided into controllers and devices (see rpt_client_type_t).

kClientProtocolEPT 

An EPT client.

EPT clients use RDMnet's extensibility to transport arbitrary manufacturer-specific data across an RDMnet broker.

kClientProtocolUnknown 

A placeholder for when a client protocol has not been determined.
